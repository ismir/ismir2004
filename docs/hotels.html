Room reservation

Hotel booking requests will be processed as soon as the Hotel Reservation Form and the required deposit have reached Events & Congresses Bureau. Rooms are assigned on a first-come, first-served basis as received by Events & Congresses Bureau and according to room availability. Should the preferred hotel be fully booked, the best alternative solutions will be offered.

Forms received after September 1st will be accepted but hotel accom-modation cannot be guaranteed.

Hotel deposits

Hotel reservation will not be confirmed if a one-night deposit has not been paid to Events & Congresses Bureau. The hotel deposit will be deducted from the final hotel bill, which delegates must settle with the hotel on checkout. The deposit is necessary in order to guarantee your reservation.

Please bear in mind that for the University Halls , the total cost of the stay must be paid in full upon making a reservation.

Confirmations will be sent after receipt of deposit/total payment.

Please include your credit card details regardless of the chosen method of payment as it is a hotel requirement.

Cancellation policy

Cancellations of bookings must be notified in writing to Events & Congresses Bureau. Refunds will be made as follows:

Before July 15th:

  

The deposit minus 30% will be refunded.

After July 15th:

  

There is a cancellation charge equivalent to a one-night room rate per room cancelled. Similarly, a one-night rate will be charge should there be a no-show.
