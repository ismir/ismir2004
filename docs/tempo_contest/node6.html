Song excerpts

A professional musician placed beat marks on several song excerpts. The ground-truth tempo was computed as the median of the inter-beat intervals.
  • Total number of instances: 465
  • Duration: ~20 s
  • Total duration: ~9300 s
  • Genres: see distribution in Table 2.

The data set, tempo annotations and outputs of all algorithms are available here.

NB: Slight changes in ground-truth annotations on November 16th 2004.
